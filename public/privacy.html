Privacy Policy

Video Shrinker · Last updated: July 8, 2026

The short version: your videos never leave your device, and no data about you is collected. Ever.

How the app works

Video Shrinker compresses videos entirely on your device, in your browser's own video encoder. There is no server that receives your files. Nothing is uploaded, transmitted, or stored anywhere other than your own device.

Data collection

  • Videos: processed locally on your device only. They are never uploaded.
  • Shared files: when you share a video to the app from another app, the file is handed to the app locally by your operating system and processed on-device, exactly like a file you picked manually.
  • Personal information: none collected. The app has no accounts, no sign-in, and no way to identify you.
  • Analytics and tracking: none. No analytics libraries, no cookies, no advertising identifiers, no crash reporting.
  • Metadata: the app offers an option to strip metadata (such as location) from your videos during conversion. This also happens entirely on your device.

Network access

The app loads its own code from the web the first time you open it and when updates are available, like any web page. After that it works offline. These requests fetch the app's own files only; they never carry your videos or any information about you.

Permissions

The app requests no special permissions. It can only access video files you explicitly choose or share to it.

Children

The app collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be published at this address with a new date at the top. Given that the app collects nothing, meaningful changes are unlikely.
